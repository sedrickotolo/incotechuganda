About Incotech

Incotech is an incubator that has been developed specifically for our chicken Incubation that hatches eggs right from local to exotic types.

Its  smart incubator for chicken eggs with real time monitoring anywhere at anytime.
Controllable through Mobile phone, web app or locally at home. 

To provide smart product that can be monitored by the end user at any location he may be so that he can have good security for his product

The aim of this innovation is  improving on the poultry industry in the country since most people can not afford expensive incubators  expecially rural famers to aid in the poultry industry.  Incotech  offers  better production in the  industry both in uburn and rural areas in the country .
